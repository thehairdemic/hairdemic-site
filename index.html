Published Findings

What Hairdemic is seeing.

Six recurring patterns shaping today’s hair industry.

001

Hair Care Is No Longer the Foundation

The industry increasingly centers the finished style while long-term hair care becomes secondary or an unclear responsibility.

002

Industry Frustration Is Growing

Clients and hairstylists are expressing many of the same frustrations across today’s industry.

003

The Client–Stylist Disconnect

Expectations, communication and trust between clients and hairstylists are becoming increasingly unclear.

004

Pricing Confusion

Pricing conversations continue to dominate the industry as services become increasingly individualized.

005

Every Chair Is Different

Each stylist now operates with unique pricing, policies, expectations and service structures.

006

The Community Shift

Many people describe today’s hair industry as feeling less community-centered than it once did.
